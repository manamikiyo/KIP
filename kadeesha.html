I knew I was worth more than just being a hair dresser, so I took the big step of applying to Year Up and now do hair as a side hustle to have money for lunch, transportation, or shopping. I couldn’t let this opportunity go by, so I continued on with it because I knew it would make me a better person and make my mom proud.

I look forward to gaining more knowledge and learning new material during intern. In addition, I look forward to going back to school for computer science and business administration when the time comes. Thanks to Year Up!
